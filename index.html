Established in 2006, Gannett Peak Technical Services is a software development and consulting firm that represents the highest point of quality.

Our number one goal is creating efficient systems for individual needs, rather than trying to fix clients’ unique challenges using one-size-fits-all, pre-built software.

We believe in thoroughly analyzing the complete user experience before arriving at a software solution.

From the beginning of every project, our team of project managers and programmers will work closely—and personally—with management and end users to understand challenges and find the best solutions.

By customizing systems from a real-world business approach, Gannett Peak develops software that works from the ground up.

We are part of your team during every phase of the project.

We understand the importance of accessibility. Our staff members, including programmers, are available at all times.

We watch as users test the systems, allowing us to see places for improvement and to make changes on demand.

We have experience working with government agencies and the private sector. Because our approach is centered on working as part of your team, we maintain long-lasting relationships with our clients.
